# Be viral in your own eyes first. Celebrate your idea of success.

Brooke Brodack challenges the conventional definition of going viral with a refreshing perspective. While her 20,000 views might not be what the internet typically considers viral, in her eyes it is absolutely viral. She encourages creators to redefine success by their own standards rather than comparing themselves to mainstream metrics.

This thoughtful clip carries one of Brooke's most empowering messages: be viral in your own eyes first. By celebrating what success means to you personally, rather than measuring against someone else's yardstick, creators can find genuine satisfaction and motivation in their journey. It is a mindset shift that more people in the content creation space could benefit from.

## Key Topics

- Redefining what "going viral" means on a personal level
- Celebrating success on your own terms
- The mindset shift from external validation to internal satisfaction
- 20,000 views as a personal viral milestone
