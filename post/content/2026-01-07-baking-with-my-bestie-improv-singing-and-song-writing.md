Brooke Brodack combines two of her passions -- improvised music and cooking -- in this delightful kitchen-set improv song. Singing from the heart of a cozy Monday baking session with her bestie, she riffs on what to make, proudly declaring "we like to keep it organic" while surveying her kitchen for inspiration. The song flows naturally from muffin mix deliberations to celebrating her perfected pancake recipe to reminiscing about last night's chicken and mashed potatoes.

The video is a charming showcase of Brooke's ability to turn everyday moments into musical entertainment. She works in references to ground venison tacos, homemade sour cream that "makes me wanna scream for more," and invites viewers to "subscribe and leave a comment and tell me what to bake." It is kitchen creativity at its most spontaneous and genuine -- the kind of content that feels like hanging out with a friend who happens to break into song about everything.
