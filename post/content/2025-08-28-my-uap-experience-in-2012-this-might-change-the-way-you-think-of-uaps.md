Brooke Brodack takes viewers back to January 2012, when she and a friend were standing on the roof of her downtown Los Angeles artist loft and witnessed something extraordinary. A beautiful golden object with forward-swept wings glided silently across the sky at the height of a glider plane. She immediately rushed to her computer to search for anything resembling what she saw, and to this day, over fourteen years later, nothing on the internet has ever matched the object she witnessed.

The story takes a fascinating twist when Brooke reveals that months later, she and her friend discovered they had seen two completely different things in that same moment — she saw a golden flying machine while he saw only an orb. This discrepancy led Brooke down years of contemplation, eventually arriving at a thought-provoking theory: what if some UAPs are not alien or government-made, but rather manifestations of our own pure potential energy taking physical form through the quantum field?

Brooke shares that whenever she feels low or lost in life, she thinks about that golden object as a symbol of strength, potential, and power. The sighting has kept her going through very difficult times. She invites viewers to consider a new framework for understanding UAPs — one that includes alien craft, man-made government objects, and personal manifestations of quantum potential energy — and asks the community to share their own experiences and theories.
