# Hablando en español, amo los idiomas del corazón. @CesarMillanOfficial

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/jG8rYnLtFbs" title="Hablando en español, amo los idiomas del corazón. @CesarMillanOfficial" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen></iframe>

In a charming departure from her usual English-language content, Brooke films an entire video in Spanish, sharing her love for what she calls "languages of the heart." Inspired by watching Cesar Millan speak Spanish on his YouTube channel, she reflects on how she learned Spanish not in a classroom but through immersion, and draws a fascinating distinction between English as a "key language" of the mind and Spanish, Sanskrit, and Hindi as languages of the heart that can express things the mind alone cannot.

Brooke announces she is opening a channel for a new Hispanic audience and invites viewers to practice Spanish together in the comments. The video is peppered with playful moments, including a story about wearing a lemon on her head while visiting her grandmother in the hospital. It is a beautiful expression of her multilingual identity and her belief that deeper human connection happens through the languages we feel, not just the ones we think in.

## Key Topics

- Speaking Spanish and language immersion
- Heart languages vs. mind languages
- Cesar Millan as inspiration
- Sanskrit, Hindi, and Spanish as expressive languages
- Building a bilingual audience
