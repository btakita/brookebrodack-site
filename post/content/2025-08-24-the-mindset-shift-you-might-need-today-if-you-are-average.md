# The mindset shift you might need today if you are "average"

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/amjaroVIIIM" title="The mindset shift you might need today if you are average" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Brooke Brodack scales her message to a global level in this empowering video, building on her recent personal discussions about narcissism and Vyapti from integral yoga. She addresses the eight billion people who wake up every day agreeing to a narrative controlled by roughly 200 people -- and challenges them to make a different choice today.

The core message is direct: you are not average. You have been conditioned to believe you are because it fits the narrative of those who want to control your life force. From the health industry to the tech industry, from Bitcoin to fossil fuels, Brooke argues that billionaires' creations are destroying the lives of ordinary people while enslaving their Vyapti -- their consciousness and life force energy -- to work for someone else's vision.

But Brooke does not stop at criticism. She paints a picture of what the world could look like if every single human being was living at their fullest potential. We would still have innovations like ChatGPT, but we would also empower and fund the solutions to the problems those innovations create. She calls this mindset shift a butterfly effect capable of changing the world toward more peace, health, harmony, and unity.
