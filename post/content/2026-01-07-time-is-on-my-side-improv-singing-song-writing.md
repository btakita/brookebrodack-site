# Time is on my side : improv singing song writing

Brooke Brodack crafts a contemplative improvised song about humanity's relationship with time. With a mellow, thoughtful melody, she sings about the paradox of time -- how it keeps ticking by, yet is somehow always on her side. The lyrics ask listeners to reflect on their own experiences: "Have you ever heard the clock tick with a heavy heart? Have you ever felt the time slowing down? Have you ever felt the speed up to the point you couldn't keep up?"

The song captures a universal feeling that many can relate to -- the way time seems to stretch during difficult moments and race during joyful ones. Brooke's improvised lyrics touch on mindfulness and presence, suggesting that our relationship with time is shaped by our inner state. It is a short but resonant piece of musical philosophy delivered in her signature spontaneous style.

## Key Topics

- Improvised songwriting exploring the human experience of time
- The paradox of time feeling both fast and slow depending on emotional state
- Mindfulness and presence in relation to time perception
