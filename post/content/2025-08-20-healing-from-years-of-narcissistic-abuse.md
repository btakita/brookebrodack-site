# Healing from years of narcissistic abuse.

Brooke Brodack continues her courageous series on healing from narcissistic abuse, acknowledging how scary and painful it is to share this information publicly while recognizing it is pivotal to her healing. She reveals how the pattern has played out in her life: starting career coaching and immediately assuming she is the problem, when in reality narcissistic people around her have created completely unwarranted drama designed to derail her at the exact moments she is about to succeed.

What makes this video especially powerful is Brooke's refusal to play the victim. After two years of studying narcissism and working with an NPD specialist, she has reached the next level -- she can see the blessing in the narcissist. She is ready to write a book about living your highest truth and purpose through that lens. Rather than chasing viral fame, Brooke values 200 organic, intentional views far more than clickbait numbers, because this content can genuinely heal people.

Brooke is transparent about the toll: waking up broke every morning and blaming herself while never holding the narcissists in her life even semi-responsible for the career derailment they caused. Coming out with this information, she believes, will help her release the blockages around her career that have held her back for years.
