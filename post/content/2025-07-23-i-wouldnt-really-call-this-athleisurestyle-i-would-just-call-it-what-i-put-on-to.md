Brooke Brodack delivers a quick, no-nonsense fashion take in this short video. After seeing a video claiming athleisure is out, she pushes back with a simple truth: she would not call her outfit athleisure -- she would just call it "what I put on today." And she thinks it makes a really strong statement on its own terms, no trend label required.

It is a brief but pointed commentary on how fashion culture loves to categorize and then discard styles, when the reality for most people is far simpler: you wear what feels right and what works for your day.

## Key Topics

- Rejecting the athleisure label in favor of personal style
- Fashion trends versus dressing authentically
- Making a strong style statement without following categories
