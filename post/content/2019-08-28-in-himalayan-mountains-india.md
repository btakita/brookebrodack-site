Brooke Brodack shares a short atmospheric clip from her time in the Himalayan mountains of India. The video lets the natural sounds and scenery speak for themselves, offering a meditative window into one of the most breathtaking landscapes on earth. This footage comes from Brooke's trek from Jaipur to Manali, a journey she undertook from October to early December 2019 on a limited budget -- an experience she described as longer and more exhaustive than anticipated, with much of the original footage and photos unfortunately lost along the way.
