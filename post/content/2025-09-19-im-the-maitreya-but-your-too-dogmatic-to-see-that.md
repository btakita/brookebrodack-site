Brooke delivers one of her most entertaining monologues, blending deep Buddhist philosophy with irreverent comedy in a way only she can. She opens by describing the "hours and hours and hours" of Dharma talks she has consumed over the past decade in pursuit of becoming a Buddha, then playfully declares herself the Maitreya -- the prophesied future Buddha -- while acknowledging that nobody recognizes her because the world is too dogmatic. She even thanks the YouTube algorithm for keeping her secret safe by suppressing her content.

The rant spirals hilariously through past life reclamation, the spiritual inability of modern people to recognize "the crazy powerful soul that I am," and her backup career options: rapper, artist, or "cow poop farmer." She takes a comedic detour into horror movies, advising fans to "stop watching horror movies and go get an exorcist," before wishing everyone a happy almost-Halloween. It is a masterclass in Brooke's unique ability to discuss genuine spiritual concepts while never taking herself too seriously.
