# Spring time in CT. GOATs are jumping ze fence!

Brooke Brodack brings her signature comedic energy to a springtime vlog in Connecticut, complete with her beloved French character narrating the rolling hills, dandelions, and sunshine. Joined by her young daughter Leilani, who steals the show demanding ice cream and fighting for camera time, Brooke captures the joyful chaos of making content as a mom who has been creating videos for two decades.

Between bites of ice cream and sips of coffee, Brooke shares her excitement about ChatGPT as a creative assistant, noting it suggested she make a "comeback" even though she never left. She revisits the goat farm from a previous video, where her homemade "goat salad" was such a hit that the goats are now jumping the fence to get more. This sparks a genuine business idea about starting a juice truck in Connecticut, where she feels healthy food options are sorely lacking.

The video is a delightful mix of Brooke's improvisational comedy, heartfelt shout-outs to fellow creator mom Sophia Saidi, and the everyday adventure of balancing parenthood with content creation. Her daughter's constant interruptions and the puppy's antics only add to the charm of this slice-of-life spring day.
