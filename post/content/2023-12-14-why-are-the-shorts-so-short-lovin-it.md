# Why are the shorts so short? Lovin it.

Brooke Brodack leans into the ultra-brief format with this short that lives up to its title. In just a few seconds, she delivers a poetic reflection on change and the enduring love of life — "Every house has changed, the love of life. It's always the love of life." The brevity is the point, and Brooke is clearly having fun with it.

This clip is part of Brooke's December 2023 burst of YouTube Shorts experimentation, where she tests just how short a short can be while still delivering something meaningful. The answer, it turns out, is very short indeed.
