# internet problem in india!

Brooke Brodack goes live in a moment of raw frustration as India's notoriously unreliable internet sabotages her ability to earn a living. After finding a friend's fiber cable connection and getting excited about busting out hours on Cambly, her laptop overheats, the screen turns off, and the whole plan crumbles. She candidly admits the stress has her so frazzled she can barely form words properly.

The video lays bare the unglamorous reality of trying to sustain a digital nomad lifestyle in a country with infrastructure challenges. Brooke discusses her dual income streams -- Cambly English tutoring and creative consulting -- and how both depend on reliable connectivity. With a hard deadline to leave India by July 10th and finances tight, she is simultaneously planning a move to Mirissa, Sri Lanka, where she hopes to decompress on the beach and rediscover that life is worth living.

Despite the stress, the video ends on a hopeful note as Brooke commits to sharing her voice and connecting with people, reminding viewers that the channel is about to get much better once she reaches a tropical paradise by early July.
