Brooke Brodack curates a delightful compilation of funny moments from dharma talks and Buddhist community gatherings, proving that the spiritual path is not without its humor. The video opens with a teacher arriving dressed as "Gandalf the Black" with a wizard staff, negotiating with the Roshi about whether he can wear the hat during the talk.

The collection includes an awkward but endearing exchange where both a teacher and student admit that the person they know who broke someone's heart through renunciation is themselves, neither quite wanting to share the details. Another monk recounts the uncomfortable situation of finding himself seated next to bikini-clad young women on a Thai beach, worrying about what the local newspapers might think.

This video highlights an often-overlooked side of Buddhist practice: the humor, vulnerability, and very human moments that arise even in the most contemplative settings. Brooke's curation reflects her deep appreciation for the dhamma while celebrating the lighter side of monastic life. Sadhu, sadhu, sadhu.
