# thrive in Changing times. Vedic astrology. Ending co dependence

In one of her most introspective videos, Brooke Brodack delivers a powerful message about ending codependency and finding true independence through self-knowledge. She speaks directly to viewers who feel stuck, disempowered, or trapped in situations where others hold power over them, urging them to recognize that the only person who can truly fulfill them is themselves. It is a deeply personal talk that draws from years of Brooke's own struggles with loneliness, isolation, and living outside conventional norms.

Brooke reveals how getting her Vedic astrological chart read was a transformative experience that surpassed anything a guru or therapist had offered her. Through the reading, she gained a cosmic blueprint of her life that made it impossible for anyone else to define her identity or destiny. She warns viewers about fraudulent astrologers while recommending the Vedic astrologer who changed her perspective, sharing that the reading shed light on over thirty years of darkness and struggle.

The video also addresses the broader cultural moment, touching on job insecurity, the importance of not living on a "high horse," and the urgent need to step into authenticity. Brooke emphasizes that while we are social beings who need connection, our primary relationship must be with ourselves, and from that foundation of self-knowledge, we can light up others and find the communities that resonate with who we truly are.
