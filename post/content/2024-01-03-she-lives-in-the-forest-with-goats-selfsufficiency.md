# She lives is the forest with goats. #selfsufficiency

<iframe width="100%" style="aspect-ratio:16/9" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Or8HNNvHTYs" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Brooke Brodack offers a brief, charming window into her self-sufficient lifestyle living close to nature with goats. She shares her preference for sitting on the ground to eat rather than at a table, using a goat as a backrest, and waking up to the sound of birds each morning rather than an alarm clock. If she were inside, she notes, she could not hear the birdsong that starts her day.

This short video captures the essence of Brooke's philosophy of simple, grounded living in harmony with animals and the natural world, a theme that runs through much of her content.

## Key Topics

- Self-sufficient living in nature with goats
- Preferring ground-level living over conventional furniture
- Waking to birdsong as a daily practice
- Simple, grounded lifestyle philosophy
