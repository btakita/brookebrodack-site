# Content creation and alcohol do not need go hand in hand.

Brooke Brodack pushes back against the pervasive idea that content creators need to drink alcohol and party hard to live a cool or aspirational life. She has a particular creator in mind and is not shy about calling out the drinking culture that so many influencers perpetuate online.

This clip is part of Brooke's broader message about sobriety and authenticity in the content creation space. She makes a compelling case that the glamorization of alcohol in creator culture is both unnecessary and harmful, and that truly cool content comes from a clear and present mind.

## Key Topics

- Challenging alcohol culture among content creators
- The myth that partying hard equals living a cool life
