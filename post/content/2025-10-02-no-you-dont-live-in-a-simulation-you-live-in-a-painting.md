# No you don't live in a #simulation, you live in a painting.

Brooke offers a refreshingly artistic rebuttal to the popular simulation theory championed by Elon Musk. Rather than accepting the cold, digital framing of reality as a computer program, she argues that we live in a painting -- one that artists have been creating and shaping for thousands of years. It is a perspective that reclaims the beauty and intentionality of human experience from the sterile language of technology.

The video is shot with Brooke's signature casual energy, filmed on the go as she walks past signs and through tunnels. The brevity of the clip only strengthens the punch of its central idea: that the world we inhabit is not a glitch in some cosmic code, but a living canvas shaped by human creativity across millennia.
