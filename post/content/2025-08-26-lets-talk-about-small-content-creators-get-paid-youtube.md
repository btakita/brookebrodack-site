# Let's talk about small content creators get PAID. #youtube

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Eg-oy2aU7Os" title="Let's talk about small content creators get PAID. #youtube" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Brooke Brodack dives deeper into her vision for a platform that connects small businesses with small content creators through a shared funding pool. She challenges the "go big or get nothing" mentality that dominates American business culture, asking why content creation has to be an all-or-nothing game where creators either clear YouTube's impossibly high bar or earn nothing at all.

The argument is compelling and ecosystem-minded. Brooke frames it as a circle of life -- when small creators are empowered and earning a living, their inspired work lifts the quality of content across the entire platform, including for larger creators. She also makes the practical case for small businesses: a local coffee shop does not need hundreds of thousands of views and could not even serve that many customers. These businesses need a more local, less polished approach that actually matches their scale.

Brooke sees a real business opportunity in the space between massive platforms and individual creators -- one that America's obsession with going big has caused everyone to overlook.
