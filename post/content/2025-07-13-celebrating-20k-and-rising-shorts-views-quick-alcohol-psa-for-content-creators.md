# Celebrating 20k+ and rising shorts views. Quick alcohol PSA for content creators.

Brooke Brodack delivers the full-length version of her celebration video, marking 20,000+ YouTube Shorts views and rising. On the suggestion of her career coach from Strawberry.me, she created this video to properly celebrate a milestone that feels truly viral to her, even if mainstream internet culture might not classify it that way. She contrasts this success with her previous Reddit viral moment of 38,000 views and 800 comments, noting that this time the views are on current, relevant content.

The video takes a meaningful turn as Brooke shares her sobriety journey. Unlike her last celebration, this one is entirely alcohol-free. She recounts how a turquoise charged Tibetan medicine pill for her liver, from Jampha.com, was the catalyst that made her quit drinking three years ago. The experience of nearly choking on that pill sealed her decision to never touch alcohol again. Brooke then delivers a direct PSA to content creators, calling out Lost LeBlanc and the broader influencer culture that glorifies drinking. She argues that creators are more productive, more energetic, and more authentically happy without alcohol. The video closes with Brooke's excitement about YouTube Shorts as a platform that finally lets small creators get discovered.
