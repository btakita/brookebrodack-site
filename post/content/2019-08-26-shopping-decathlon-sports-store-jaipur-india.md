# Shopping @Decathlon Sports store Jaipur, INDIA

Brooke Brodack documents a full day of trek preparation in Jaipur, India, vlogging the experience of shopping for essential gear at a Decathlon sports store with a local friend. The day begins with booking an OLA car, hitting up a Union Bank ATM, and then heading to Decathlon to buy backpacks and twenty-dollar running shoes for the ambitious Himalayan trek ahead -- a journey from Jaipur to Manali that would span October through early December 2019.

The vlog captures the vibrant chaos of Jaipur streets, cafe stops, improving Hindi skills, and the simple joy of feeling like a real backpacker for the first time. Brooke and her friend test their new gear with a 12-kilometer walk through the city as the sun sets, passing curious onlookers who have never seen a foreigner change shoes on the street. The day ends with Brooke reflecting on the energy it takes to film and travel simultaneously, knowing they will wake at 3 AM the next morning to begin the real journey.
