# Regulating your nervous system post partum

Brooke Brodack addresses an aspect of postpartum life that she feels is vastly underrepresented in public conversation: nervous system regulation after giving birth. Drawing from her own experience as a mother, she emphasizes just how much stress the nervous system endures during and after childbirth, and how important it is for new mothers to prioritize their own healing.

In this short but impactful video, Brooke highlights the gap in postpartum resources and discussions around nervous system health. She encourages mothers to recognize the toll that birth takes on the body's stress response systems and to seek out practices that support regulation and recovery during the postpartum period.

## Key Topics

- The importance of nervous system regulation after childbirth
- How postpartum stress is underrepresented in mainstream conversations
- The physical and emotional toll of giving birth on the nervous system
- Encouraging new mothers to prioritize their own healing
