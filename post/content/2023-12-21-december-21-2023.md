"This is my favorite show, because it is my show. If this was your show, you'd probably like it more than I did. But it's not your show." The logic spirals in on itself in the best possible way.

This clip is a throwback to the kind of rapid-fire, stream-of-consciousness comedy that made Brooke famous in the first place. Fans of Tom Green and early YouTube comedy will recognize the energy — it is Brookers at her most unapologetically weird and wonderful.
