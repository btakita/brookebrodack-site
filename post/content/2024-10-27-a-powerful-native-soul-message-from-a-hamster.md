# A Powerful NATIVE soul message from a Hamster.

<iframe width="100%" style="aspect-ratio:16/9" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/kXTh9mpEb-w" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>

In one of her most emotionally raw and spiritually profound videos, Brooke Brodack processes the loss of her Siberian dwarf hamster after two and a half years together. She originally bought the hamster to teach her daughter kindness after an incident involving a thrown plastic cow, and the lesson worked perfectly. But what Brooke did not expect was how deeply this tiny creature would impact her own understanding of life, death, and the sacred nature of all beings.

Brooke shares an astonishing realization: despite being an avid photographer who documents everything in her life, she somehow never took a single photograph or video of this hamster. Rather than seeing this as an oversight, Brooke interprets it through a Native American spiritual lens as evidence of a wise soul who came into her life with a specific purpose. After searching her phone fruitlessly for a photo, she felt guided to look at her teabag, where the message read: "In order to be remembered, leave nothing behind but love."

The video weaves together Buddhism, Native American spirituality, and Brooke's practice as a Vipassana meditator into a meditation on impermanence and unconditional love. She reflects on how animals embody this principle naturally, leaving nothing behind but love in every life, and how this simple hamster taught her more about the sacred nature of existence than she could have imagined. Through tears, Brooke delivers a moving tribute that reminds viewers to love all beings, big and small, because you never know what kind of soul is embodied in even the smallest creature.
