Brooke Brodack uses her Cambly free trial to connect with John, a fellow American English tutor, for a surprisingly information-rich conversation about ESL teaching. What starts as a casual check-in quickly becomes a masterclass in online teaching resources. John shares a wealth of professional knowledge, from Cambridge University's English education standards and bilingual dictionaries to the Off2Class lesson planning platform, giving Brooke practical tools to improve her Cambly teaching sessions.

The conversation takes a personal turn as both share their experiences living abroad. Brooke talks about her time in India, Bali, Malaysia, and the Himalayan mountains, while John recounts seven years in Korea, five in the Middle East, four in Portugal, and two in Germany. When Brooke mentions being one of the first YouTubers to get famous and her nine-month NBC development deal, the connection between her past in entertainment and her present as a traveling English teacher creates a compelling portrait of reinvention.
