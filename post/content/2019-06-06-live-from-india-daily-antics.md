Brooke Brodack goes live from Auroville, India to experiment with a new format: capturing the daily antics of her life abroad in real time. After finally securing a decent internet connection via Jio (India's popular mobile dongle), she is excited to try live streaming as an alternative to the pre-recorded footage she had been shooting but finding unsatisfying.

The stream quickly becomes a comedic exercise in Murphy's Law -- the moment Brooke goes live, absolutely nothing happens. She sits in a corporate office space watching a sunset, noting the irony that all the interesting moments seem to vanish when the camera is rolling live. The brief video ends with Brooke deciding to relocate and restart, but not before asking viewers for recommendations on the best live streaming tools and expressing her search for sponsors and investors for the new channel.
