# I cracked. To much Sirius biz ness

Brooke opens with the declaration, "I'm cracking. Not cracking like the sea monster, like mentally," and proceeds to deliver a cathartic vent about the tension between trying to be smart and serious versus embracing her authentic, playful self. She describes the exhausting cycle of attempting to match the intellectual standards of the world while feeling dragged down by collective ignorance, and decides the solution might be a three-mile treadmill run for clarity and personal power.

The video takes a wonderfully surreal turn when Brooke attributes her feelings to eclipse season energy and channels her frustration into the now-iconic phrase "serious blubble blubble blubble." She bounces between moments of genuine introspection about why she will "never be an important influential figure" because she cannot stay serious, and deadpan observations about aliens with "soul evaporating technologies." It is a perfectly chaotic snapshot of a creative mind rebelling against the pressure to conform.
