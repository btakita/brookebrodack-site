Brooke Brodack kicks off a playful new mini vlog series called "Outfit of the Day," filmed at a Kansas municipal RV park in the fall of 2020. Going makeup-free to make a bold statement, she breaks down her fall look with the comedic flair that has defined her content since the early days of YouTube. The outfit -- a red sweatshirt paired with gray fleece-lined leggings -- gets the full fashion critique treatment as Brooke channels her inner runway commentator.

The video blends Brooke's signature humor with genuine style insight, drawing on her experience teaching video production and fashion classes to college students in India. She describes the look as "western casual" with celebrity-at-the-gas-pump energy, accessorized by her Bernedoodle companion and a pair of handmade Navajo turquoise and silver earrings. It is a lighthearted reminder that fashion can be both effortless and expressive.
