# Ascend Collective, Making + Meaning

Brooke Brodack goes live to share a wave of exciting updates after a long hiatus from streaming. The video covers two major developments in her creative life: joining Elise Parker's Ascend Collective -- a free VIP Facebook group with live Zoom calls focused on life coaching and alignment -- and enrolling in SCI-Arc's Making + Meaning course, an online architecture program she had wanted to take for eight years.

Brooke describes how Elise Parker's work inspired her while she was in Taos, and how the Ascend Collective's focus on confidence and purpose aligns with her own journey. She floats the idea of creating her own free VIP Facebook group centered around YouTube creativity and video creation. On the SCI-Arc front, she dives into learning Twinmotion software and shares her excitement about bridging the worlds of architecture, digital design, and content creation.

The live stream also touches on a Business Insider article about early YouTubers, her new gimbal purchase, and her honest struggle with self-sabotage when things start going well. Through it all, Brooke maintains an infectious optimism about closing gaps and stepping into the reality she knows she is meant to inhabit.
