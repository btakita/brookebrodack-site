# 2020 The Year of True Beauty

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Ssh2GyjChOo" title="2020 The Year of True Beauty" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Brooke Brodack delivers a deeply personal live stream about the merging of inner and outer beauty, and why she believes 2020 will be a transformative year. She opens up about spending years deliberately running away from physical beauty -- hiding behind comedy, nerdiness, and self-deprecation -- rather than embracing it. A turning point came in 2012 when she met a group of women from different backgrounds who helped her see beauty from the inside out for the first time.

The video becomes an unflinching look at eight years of taking the back seat in her own life, watching people interact with a version of herself that was not fully authentic. Brooke talks about the fire inside her that wants to shout at the world for everything done wrong, and how meditation has taught her to tame that impulse. She connects her time teaching on Cambly -- earning 17 to 20 cents a minute -- to the humbling process of rebuilding, while declaring it is time to step into what she knows she is truly capable of.

The stream ends with a call to evolve quickly, shed old layers, and become the greatest version of herself -- a message Brooke extends to everyone watching as 2020 approaches.

## Key Topics

- The merging of inner beauty and outer beauty
- Running from physical beauty through comedy and self-deprecation
- The 2012 turning point with a diverse group of women
- Eight years of self-discovery and taking the back seat in her own life
- Teaching on Cambly as a humbling experience
- Meditation as a tool for managing anger and frustration
- Stepping into full power for 2020
