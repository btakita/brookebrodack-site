# Exploring the realm of the "unknown" and pure potential energy.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/pHRQi1X9jok" title="Exploring the realm of the unknown and pure potential energy." frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Brooke Brodack takes viewers into one of the most transformative chapters of her life -- her decision to step into the unknown in India in 2019. After years of traveling back and forth looking for something, she reached a breaking point. Tired of how the world perceived her, she bought a bag and set out walking with no destination, putting full faith in God and in her yogic self. That journey carried her from Jaipur to Manali over three to four months, guided by courage, the beauty of nature, and connections with others.

What Brooke discovered in the unknown surprised even her. The realm of the unknown, she explains, is not really for creation -- it is for clearing. It clears old conditionings, outdated ideas of who we are, and limiting beliefs about what we are capable of. Whether that journey is an inward meditation or an outward physical trek like Brooke's walking meditation through the Himalayas, the purpose is the same: to strip away everything that is not authentically ours.

Brooke addresses this message not only to average people but also to the wealthy, challenging them to consider that even their 100-year plans might be more outdated than they think. She hopes everyone will find their unique pure potential energy and discover that they might not be living their life's greatest purpose.
